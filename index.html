01
Can I work on my existing projects during a hackathon?

No, we will accept only fresh projects.

02
What should I bring to a hackathon?

Bring whatever coincides with our themes.

03
Do I need to have a team to participate, or can I join as an individual?

A team can have 2-4 members or you can also participate individually.

04
Can anyone participate in a hackathon?

Yes, any college student can participate.

05
Is there a parking facility available for participants?

No, bring vehicles at your own risk.

06
Can I use open-source libraries or frameworks in my project?

Yes, but don't only build on top of that; use your own code too.

07
Are there any restrictions on the programming languages or technologies I can use?

No

08
Can I use hardware or external devices in my project?

Yes

09
Is there a particular theme in the hackathon?

No , There will be no particular themes , the hackathon will be open innovation.
